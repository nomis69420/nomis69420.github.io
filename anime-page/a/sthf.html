Senko The Helpful Fox is an anime about a demi-god fox and a guy who is overworked and stressed out from work and haves this darkness thing growing from him. So demi-god fox is sent down to help him and from this point on to the last 2 episodes is just wholesome interaction and just a feel-good Senko is unbelievable cute from her cute face down to her big fluffy tail. Last two episode is where it seems like Senko-san will be going back where she came from and at the end she doesn't and continues to stay with the man.

As I said it is a wholesome feel-good anime gave it a 7/10 on MAL don't think i'll rewatch this show, maybe if I want to rewatch it for the wholesomeness again #SenkoGangOnTop.
